Did you know poker machines don't have losing sounds? Even players don't know.




So we modified a poker machine and tested losing sounds on real gamers. Have a look.


It really work to reduce harm.





We're urging government to enforce losing sounds in all poker machines.

Why did we do this?
Over one year, a close relative lost 150k on the pokies. They slapped away the credit cards, the childrenâ€™s bank accounts, and the entire house deposit. It broke their family apart. And I became very angry, until recently when I overhear the pokies at a pub. There was not a single losing sound coming out of them. It changed my view. I was less angry at the person and more angry at the machines. Pokies are designed to be addictive. Adding losing sounds is one small way to change that.
