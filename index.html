Did you know poker machines don't have losing sounds? Even players don't know.








So we modified a poker machine and tested losing sounds on real gamers. Have a look.






It really works to reduce harm.

Why did we do this?

A close relative of ours lost 150k on the pokies. In the space of one year they slapped away the credit cards, the childrenâ€™s bank accounts, the entire house deposit, and broke their family apart. This is one of thousands of stories all aided by highly addictive poker machines in pubs. While the East Sydney Hotel has always been proudly pokie-free, we still want to minimise the harm pokies can do on individuals.
